From the North Country

Cold water is where it starts — and where the good stuff stays clean.

Adirondack Cold Water is solventless live rosin made the honest way: fresh-frozen flower, ice-cold water, and gentle heat and pressure. No butane. No solvents. Nothing between you and the plant.

Inspired by the clear, cold waters of the Adirondacks, every batch is small, hand-pressed, and made to preserve the full flavor and character of the living plant. Strong when you want it, clean every time.

How It’s Made

Ice, Water & Pressure

Four steps from living plant to golden rosin — and not a single solvent in sight.

Fresh Frozen

Flower is harvested and frozen at peak ripeness to lock in the living terpene profile.

Ice Water Wash

Cold agitation separates the trichome heads — pure resin — from the plant material.

Dried Hash

The collected hash is carefully sifted and freeze-dried to protect its delicate compounds.

Rosin Press

Gentle heat and pressure release the oil — live rosin, solventless and complete.
